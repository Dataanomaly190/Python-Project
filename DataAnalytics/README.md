# Data Science & Analytics Collection

A collection of high-impact data analysis projects leveraging the **NumPy-Pandas-Matplotlib** stack to derive business intelligence and socioeconomic insights.

## Featured Projects

### 1. [Bank Marketing Campaign](./BankData_Analysis/)

- **Domain**: Finance / Marketing.
- **Focus**: Analyzing term deposit subscription factors.
- **Highlights**: Full Streamlit dashboard with recursive filters.

### 2. [Books Analysis & Scoring](./BooksAnalysis/)

- **Domain**: Literature / Metadata.
- **Focus**: Building custom weighted popularity & quality scores for 10k+ books.
- **Highlights**: Rolling average trend lines and rating decay models.

### 3. [Adult Census Income Report](./CensusReport/)

- **Domain**: Socioeconomics / Demographic Research.
- **Focus**: Analyzing the drivers of high-income earners.
- **Highlights**: Statistical summaries and demographic probability heatmaps.

---

## General Tech Stack

- **Languages**: Python
- **Engine**: NumPy (Numerical Processing), Pandas (Data Manipulation)
- **Visualization**: Matplotlib, Seaborn
- **Interaction**: Streamlit (Interactive Dashboards)
